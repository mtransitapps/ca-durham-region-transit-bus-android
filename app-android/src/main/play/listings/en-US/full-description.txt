This app adds Durham Region Transit (DRT) buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule as well as the latest news from @Durham_Transit on Twitter.

DRT buses serve the Durham Region in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Region of Durham.
https://opendata.durham.ca/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-durham-region-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Durham Region Transit (DRT) or the Region of Durham.

Permissions:
- Other: required to news from Twitter
